Section 01

Roadmap Overview & Cadence

2–3
Posts per month
3

800–1,500

The cadence is designed for a one-person operation. Two to three posts per month is achievable for Haqq without burning out, and it's enough to build meaningful SEO momentum over 12 months. Consistency matters more than volume — publishing 2 posts every month beats publishing 8 posts in January and then nothing for three months.

Ramadan Priority

The calendar front-loads Ramadan content. Since Ramadan is Zim Zam's peak relevance window — when Muslims are most conscious about hydration, wellness, and spiritual practices — the first 2–3 months include content specifically designed to rank before Ramadan arrives. Posts published in Month 1–2 will have time to get indexed and start ranking before the seasonal search spike.

A note on timing

This roadmap uses "Month 1, Month 2..." rather than specific calendar months because it starts whenever Haqq launches the new site. If launch happens in April 2026, Month 1 is April. The Ramadan content should be republished and refreshed 6 weeks before Ramadan each year regardless of when the initial launch happens — it's an annual content asset.

Section 04

Writing & Publishing Workflow

Haqq is a one-person operation. The workflow needs to be realistic and sustainable.

Recommended workflow

  • 1 Outline (15 min): Write 5–7 bullet points covering the main ideas. Use the target keyword as the H1 or a close variation.
  • 2 Draft (45–60 min): Write the post from the outline. Don't aim for perfection — aim for clarity. 800–1,500 words. Use short paragraphs, subheadings, and bullet points for scannability.
  • 3 Optimize (15 min): Add the meta title and meta description. Add alt text to any images. Add 2–3 internal links to other pages on the site. Make sure the target keyword appears in the H1, first paragraph, and at least one subheading.
  • 4 Publish and share (10 min): Publish the post. Share on Instagram and TikTok with a summary or quote graphic. Email subscribers if list exists.

Total time per post: ~90 minutes. That's two posts per month at under 3 hours/month of writing time. Achievable.

AI Writing Assist

Haqq can use AI tools (like ChatGPT) to speed up drafting — but the final voice must be his. AI can generate outlines, suggest structures, and help with research summaries, but the personal stories, Islamic references, and brand voice need to come from Haqq. The audience will know the difference between authentic content and generic AI output.

Section 06

Content Repurposing Plan

Every blog post should be repurposed into social media content. One blog post can generate 3–5 pieces of social content with minimal extra effort.

From one blog post, create:

  • • Instagram carousel: Pull 5–7 key facts or tips from the post, turn each into a slide. This is the highest-performing organic format on Instagram for educational content.
  • • TikTok/Reel: Haqq talking to camera, sharing the one most surprising fact from the post. 30–60 seconds. Link to the full post in bio.
  • • Quote graphic: One compelling stat or sentence from the post, designed as a shareable graphic. "Zamzam water contains 25x more calcium than tap water."
  • • Email snippet: When the email list is built, each post becomes a newsletter topic. 3-sentence summary + "Read the full post" link.
  • • Community post: Share the blog post link with a personal comment in Muslim Facebook groups, Reddit (r/islam, r/muslimlounge), and other community spaces where Haqq is already present. Don't spam — add value, share the link naturally.
The Multiplier Effect

2 blog posts per month × 4 social pieces each = 8 pieces of content per month. This creates a consistent content presence across blog (SEO) + Instagram + TikTok + email without requiring 8 separate creative sessions. Write the blog post first, then slice it up. The blog is the content factory; social media is the distribution channel.

Blog Content Roadmap Summary

30 posts over 12 months. 12 Pillar 1 (Zamzam education), 11 Pillar 2 (Muslim wellness), 7 Pillar 3 (brand & community).

The first 3 months are the most important. The foundation posts (Zamzam water overview, Ramadan hydration guide, founder story) establish the blog's identity and begin the SEO clock. Every month of indexing makes future content stronger.

Ramadan is the annual peak. Refresh the hydration guide every year, publish seasonal content 6 weeks before, and ride the wave. This is the one time of year when "hydration" and "Muslim" intersect in search at massive scale.

~90 minutes per post. Realistic for a solo founder. Consistency beats volume. Two solid posts per month, every month, for 12 months will transform Zim Zam's search visibility.
